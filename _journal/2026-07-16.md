## Supervised connection generation

- Added `SupervisedClient::connection_generation()`, starting at one and incrementing only after a
  reconnect has registered and replayed all recorded subscriptions.
- This gives resident consumers a lossless reconnect signal; polling `ConnState` alone can miss a
  fast disconnect/reconnect between samples.
- Pinned the behaviour in the supervised reconnect integration test. Workspace tests and clippy
  pass under `memguard`.
